Kallies Kosmetics!

Kadence Kallie is a six-year-old creative fashionista taking the industry by storm. Those close refer to her as, Miss KK, always had a passion for fashion and beauty products. It became second nature for Kadence to appear out if nowhere with several different colors which we now refer to it as ombre, lipstick around her lips, almost touching her nose, and sometimes over her eyes. At first glance, I was struck by the way she interpreted her colors. I then went on and effortlessly grabbed wipes and gently washed her face. I can recall one encounter when she looked up at me with her bright eyes and in a barely audible whimper, she said, Mommy I want my own Lipstick.After a year of research, meetings and emails, here we are today launching Kallie Kosmetics, that offer kid friendly lip gloss and nail polish. I went the extra mile to make sure the chemicals were non-toxic, and proven safe. When this confident cutie isn’t ripping the runway, she’s sitting along side her Mommy giving her spill on ideas for her upcoming collection. Kallie Kosmetics is said to become a staple brand all over the world..
